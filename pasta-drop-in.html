Pasta With Erika

Thursday public drop-in. Learn. Create. Eat together.

Learn. Create. Eat together.

Join Erika for a relaxed, hands-on pasta lab where you'll make, cook, and enjoy your own traditional Italian pasta in an informal and welcoming setting.

Guests may arrive anytime between 5:00 PM and 8:00 PM and participate at their own pace. On Thursdays, we focus on traditional Italian pasta made with semolina or potatoes, celebrating regional shapes such as Orecchiette, Cavatelli, Fusilloni, bucatini-style pasta, and Potato Gnocchi.

No previous experience is required.

Thursday FAQ

Pasta With Erika is a relaxed, hands-on Thursday pasta lab where you make, cook, and enjoy your own traditional Italian pasta in an informal and welcoming setting. Guests may arrive anytime between 5 PM and 8 PM and participate at their own pace.

On Thursdays we focus on traditional Italian pasta made with semolina or potatoes, celebrating regional shapes — Orecchiette, Cavatelli, Fusilloni, bucatini-style pasta, and Potato Gnocchi. No previous experience required.

Pasta With Erika is open to adults, couples, families, and children. Come with an open heart, an appetite, and a willingness to get your hands a little floury.

Absolutely. The Thursday session is relaxed and welcoming for all ages, families, and solo guests alike — exactly the kind of easy weeknight gathering we love to share.
